Travel to Switzerland

Switzerland, officially the Swiss Confederation, is a landlocked country located at the confluence of Western, Central, and Southern Europe. It is known for its mountains, with the Alps covering most of the country. Switzerland is famous for its breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and historical cities, making it a popular tourist destination.
